Formula Reference & Theory

For a tweeter operating in the mass-controlled region (f ≫ Fs), peak cone displacement is:

x_peak = (Bl × V_rms × √2) / (Re × Mms × (2πf)²)

Where:

  • Bl — force factor (T·m)
  • V_rms — RMS voltage across driver (V)
  • Re — DC voice coil resistance (Ω)
  • Mms — total moving mass (kg)
  • f — frequency (Hz)

The maximum safe voltage at a given frequency (before Xmax is exceeded):

V_rms_max = Xmax × Re × Mms × (2πf)² / (Bl × √2)

The minimum safe frequency at a given voltage:

f_min = √( Bl × V_rms × √2 / (Re × Mms × (2π)² × Xmax) )

Note: This model assumes the driver is fully mass-controlled (ω ≫ ω₀). Results are most accurate well above Fs. Near or below Fs, acoustic loading and compliance effects become significant.
